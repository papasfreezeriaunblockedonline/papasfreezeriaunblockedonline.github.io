Understanding the Stations

  • Order Station: Take customer orders and keep track of tickets.
  • Build Station: Choose the correct cup size and add ice cream and mixables.
  • Mix Station: Blend the sundae to the desired consistency.
  • Top Station: Add whipped cream, syrups, and toppings as per the order.

Tips & Strategies

  • Be Quick but Accurate: Speed is important, but accuracy ensures better tips.
  • Familiarize with Toppings: Knowing the layout of toppings can save time.
  • Keep Track of Tickets: Always match the sundae to the correct order ticket to avoid mistakes.
