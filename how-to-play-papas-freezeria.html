8. Who are the "Closers" and how do you manage them?

"Closers" are special customers who arrive last each day, one for each day of the week (e.g., Quinn on Thursday, Kahuna on Monday in some versions). They are pickier and grade orders more strictly. To manage them, check the customer tab to see who's coming and prepare their order ahead of time while serving the last few regular customers.

14. What genre of game is Papa's Freezeria?

It's primarily classified as a business simulation game and a time management game. It requires managing resources, completing tasks (preparing orders) to earn virtual currency, and optimizing time to satisfy customers.

15. How do customer ranks/stars work?

By serving high-quality orders to a customer, you earn stars. Accumulating stars promotes the customer to Bronze, Silver, and Gold ranks. Higher-ranked customers give larger tips and more points when served, contributing to overall game progression.
